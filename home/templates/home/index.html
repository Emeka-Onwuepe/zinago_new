Our Human Resource Services

Our employment lawyers offer legal assistance to Nigerian and International clients to help them achieve their business goals and conform with Nigerian employment law. We provide plans, advice, and techniques to prevent possible disruptive employee disputes. In case of legal disagreements, we vigorously defend our clients' right in court and explore other options such as mediation and arbitration.

We also offer advanced employment law guidance covering various aspects such as management and workforce terminations, employment contract negotiation and review, employee share schemes, Training and Development, Recruitment and Selection, outsourcing, pensions, tax liabilities, independent contractors, and compliance with safety and health regulations. Drafting and Review of Employment agreement, Employment Policy and Employment Handbook. We provide guidance on the immigration and tax status of foreign employees in Nigeria.
